# אורות התוהו — Lights of Tohu

**The Overwhelming Divine Lights of Olam HaTohu in Hemshech Taarav**

---

## Overview

The *orot* (lights) of Tohu are qualitatively the most powerful and intense divine illuminations in the entire structure of the spiritual worlds. In Hemshech Taarav, the Rashab devotes considerable attention to analyzing the nature of these lights — precisely because their intensity is the key to understanding both the tragedy of the Shevirat HaKelim (Breaking of the Vessels) and the hidden benefit of that breaking.

---

## Why the Tohu Lights Were So Powerful

The orot of Tohu derived their intensity from their proximity to the source. The world of Tohu emerged directly from the activation of the *Reshimu* by the *Kav* — close to the primordial moment of creation and therefore carrying the most direct possible expression of Or Ein Sof within the post-Tzimtzum framework.

Specifically, the Rashab identifies three qualities that made the Tohu lights overwhelming:

**1. Absence of Partzuf structure**: In Tikkun, the lights are distributed across integrated configurations (Partzufim) where Chochma's light is received through Bina before reaching the Middot, and so on. In Tohu, each sefirah received light *directly* — no mediation, no graduated distribution. Each light was at full intensity for its level.

**2. Each sefirah expressing its entire being**: In Tikkun, each sefirah contains a reflection of all ten sefirot within itself — this creates balance and integration. In Tohu, each sefirah expressed only its own unique quality fully, without the moderating influence of the other sefirot.

**3. Or Yashar dominance without Or Chozer**: The orot of Tohu were almost entirely Or Yashar — the downward flow of divine energy — with minimal Or Chozer (reflective, upward-returning light). This is because the Tohu-vessels, standing alone, could not generate significant Or Chozer. The result was a one-directional flood of energy with no return valve.

---

## The Paradox: Destruction Enabling the Highest Quality

The Rashab's most striking teaching about the Tohu lights: **their very excess and destructive power is what makes them, after the breaking, the most valuable spiritual material**.

When the vessels shattered, the sparks (*nitzotzot*) that fell into the lower worlds carried the quality of these supremely intense Tohu lights. These sparks, now embedded in physical creation, are of higher quality than any light that Tikkun alone could generate. This is why the effort to elevate the *nitzotzot* — the central task of all Jewish spiritual work — is of such ultimate importance.

The lights of Tohu "became" the sparks of the highest possible divine radiance, available to be elevated through Torah and mitzvot.

See: [[themes/tohu-and-tikkun/nitzotzot|Nitzotzot — The Divine Sparks]].

---

## The Tohu Lights and Human Souls

The Rashab connects the Tohu lights to a specific type of human soul — the *nefashot d'Tohu* (souls of Tohu). These souls carry within them something of the quality of the Tohu lights: immense spiritual intensity, power, and potential — but also the tendency toward extremism and difficulty with containment that characterized the Tohu world.

Great spiritual revolutionaries and those capable of *mesirut nefesh* (self-sacrifice) at the highest level tend to be souls rooted in Tohu.
