# עולם התוהו ועולם התיקון — Worlds of Chaos and Repair

**Hub page for the Tohu and Tikkun theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The Tohu/Tikkun framework is one of the most extensively developed themes in Hemshech Taarav. The Rashab uses it to explain the history of spiritual reality, the nature of the soul, the purpose of Torah and mitzvot, and the trajectory of all creation toward its ultimate rectification.
