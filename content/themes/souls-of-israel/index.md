# נשמות ישראל — Souls of Israel

**Hub page for the Souls of Israel theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The nature of Jewish souls is one of the most extensively analyzed topics in Hemshech Taarav. The Rashab provides an unprecedented account of the soul's divine root, its five levels, its relationship to the Sefirot, and its cosmic mission.
