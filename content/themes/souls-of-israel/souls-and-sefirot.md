# נשמות וספירות — Souls and Sefirot

**The Correspondence Between Souls and Sefirot in Hemshech Taarav**

---

## Overview

One of the richest structural analyses in Hemshech Taarav is the systematic mapping between the five levels of the Jewish soul and the ten Sefirot (and the Partzufim that contain them). The Rashab does not treat this correspondence as merely symbolic or analogical — it reflects a genuine ontological connection: the soul's various levels *are* expressions of specific Sefirot within the divine structure.

---

## The Primary Correspondence

The most fundamental correspondence in the Taarav:

| Soul Level | Divine Structural Level |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Yechida | Keter / Adam Kadmon |
| Chaya | Chochma / Atzilut (Abba) |
| Neshama | Bina / Atzilut (Imma) |
| Ruach | Ze'ir Anpin / Yetzira |
| Nefesh | Malchut / Asiya |

This correspondence means:
- The *Yechida* partakes of the quality of *Keter* — divine will that transcends all differentiation
- The *Chaya* partakes of the quality of *Chochma* — the first flash of divine wisdom
- The *Neshama* partakes of the quality of *Bina* — developed divine understanding
- The *Ruach* partakes of the quality of *Ze'ir Anpin* — divine emotion (love, fear, joy)
- The *Nefesh* partakes of the quality of *Malchut* — divine sovereignty present in the physical world

---

## Implications for Avodah

The Rashab draws practical conclusions from this correspondence:

**Understanding one's spiritual challenges through one's sefirah-root**: A person whose primary soul-connection is in *Chesed* (within Ze'ir Anpin's Ruach level) will struggle most with *Gevurah* — with boundaries, discipline, and saying no. Understanding this is the beginning of targeted avodah.

**The ChaBaD path**: The Chabad emphasis on intellectual meditation (*hisponenut*) targets specifically the *Neshama*-level (Bina) — deepening the Bina connection in the soul, which then draws the Chochma-connection of the Chaya level closer to consciousness.

**Emotional refinement**: The work of refining the *midot* (emotional attributes) targets the *Ruach* level — bringing Ze'ir Anpin's qualities into proper balance within the soul.

---

## Each Soul Containing All Ten Sefirot

The Rashab develops an important nuance: while each soul has a primary sefirah-connection, every soul contains *all ten Sefirot* within it (just as each sefirah contains all ten within itself). This means:

- A primarily-Chesed soul still has Gevurah within it (though less dominant)
- A primarily-Chochma soul still has Bina within it
- etc.

The spiritual work of any individual is thus both to *develop their primary sefirah-quality* to its fullest and to *balance it with the other sefirot* within their soul. This produces the integrated personality that the Rashab calls the model of *Tikkun* at the human level.
