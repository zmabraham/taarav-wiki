# Nefesh: The Foundation Level of the Soul

## English Explanation

*Nefesh* (נֶפֶשׁ) is the lowest and most accessible of the five soul-levels described
in the Hemshech Taarav, corresponding primarily to the World of Asiyah (action) and to
the body's vital, animating functions. The Nefesh is what gives the body its basic
life-force and its connection to the physical world. In the Kabbalistic scheme, Nefesh
is the soul-level that is most fully "clothed" within the body — the most dense and
concrete level of the divine soul's presence in the physical.

In the context of spiritual development, Nefesh corresponds to *action* — the most
basic level of divine service. Before a person engages their emotions or intellect in
the service of God, they must first bring their actions into alignment with divine will.
The Rashab teaches that even a person with no developed emotional or intellectual
connection to God can still perform mitzvot (commandments) through the Nefesh level —
and this is the foundation upon which higher spiritual development is built.

The Nefesh is also associated with the cycle of physical life: birth, sustenance, and
death. When the Torah speaks of the "life of all flesh" being in the blood (כִּי נֶפֶשׁ
כָּל בָּשָׂר דָּמוֹ הִוא, Leviticus 17:14), it is the Nefesh-level soul that circulates
through the blood. This is also why certain laws related to blood in Jewish law are
connected to the sanctity of life at the Nefesh level.

The Rashab notes that even Nefesh carries an infinite divine source, since it is
a portion of the infinite God. But at the Nefesh level, this infinity is most deeply
concealed within the physical structure of the body.

## Hebrew Key Terms

| Term | Transliteration | Definition |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| נֶפֶשׁ | Nefesh | Foundation soul-level; animates the body |
| עֲשִׂיָּה | Asiyah | World of Action; corresponds to Nefesh |
| דָּם | Dam | Blood; the physical medium of Nefesh |
| מַעֲשֶׂה | Maase | Action; the primary mode of Nefesh-service |
| הִתְלַבְּשׁוּת | Hitlabshut | Clothing; Nefesh is most "clothed" in the body |
