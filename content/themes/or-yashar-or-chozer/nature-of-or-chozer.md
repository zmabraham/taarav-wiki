# טבע האור חוזר — Nature of Or Chozer

**The Reflected and Returning Light in Hemshech Taarav**

---

## Overview

**Or Chozer** (אור חוזר — "returning" or "reflected" light) is the upward-directed divine illumination that arises in response to the Or Yashar. The term *chozer* (חוזר — returning, going back) indicates its directionality: whereas Or Yashar descends from Ein Sof toward creation, Or Chozer ascends from creation back toward Ein Sof.

The Rashab in Hemshech Taarav treats Or Chozer as perhaps the most significant concept in the entire hemshech — because it represents the possibility of a genuine response from below, an ascent from within the finite back to the Infinite. Developed extensively in the early maamarim of חה"ש תער"ב, Or Chozer is the theological key to the Rashab's understanding of why the world was created.

---

## Why Or Chozer Requires Tzimtzum

A central insight of the Taarav: **Or Chozer can only exist because of the Tzimtzum**. Before the Tzimtzum, the Or Ein Sof filled all existence — there was no "place" for anything else, no room for a created being to arise and generate a response. The Tzimtzum created the chalal (space) precisely so that Or Chozer could emerge.

This is a radical reframing: the Tzimtzum is not a withdrawal or absence but the *enabling condition* for the most profound divine desire — that created beings should return to and reveal the Infinite of their own volition. The Rashab develops this in contrast to earlier formulations that present Tzimtzum primarily as concealment.

---

## The Rashab's Distinctive Analysis

**1. Or Chozer as the Response of the Kli (Vessel)**
When the Or Yashar strikes a *kli* (vessel/sefirah), the kli's response — its ability to receive and reflect — generates the Or Chozer. The Or Chozer does not come from above; it is generated from below, from the interaction of divine light with a finite receiver. This is its unique dignity: it originates in the created being.

**2. Or Chozer as Higher Than Or Yashar**
Paradoxically, the Rashab teaches that Or Chozer reaches *higher* than Or Yashar. Or Yashar emanates from Ein Sof but is a graduated, sequential light — by definition it becomes more contracted as it descends. Or Chozer, arising from below and ascending upward, actually reaches the level of the *Atzmut* (divine essence) — because the yearning of the created being to return touches something that transcends the mere "output" of Ein Sof.

This is one of the Rashab's most stunning contributions: the light of response from below can reach higher than the light from above that generated it.

**3. Or Chozer and the Soul's Avodah**
In the human context, Or Chozer is the equivalent of *avodah* (divine service) — particularly prayer, Torah study undertaken with self-nullification, and the performance of mitzvot with inner intent. The Rashab teaches that authentic avodah doesn't merely "use" the Or Yashar one receives; it generates a genuine Or Chozer — an ascent that carries the created world back toward its divine source.

---

## Or Chozer in the Breaking of the Vessels

A crucial analysis in the Taarav: *Olam HaTohu* shattered precisely because the vessels could not generate adequate Or Chozer. The Or Yashar was overwhelming; the responsive capacity of the Tohu-vessels was insufficient. Or Chozer requires a stable, receptive kli — and the sefirot of Tohu, standing alone as isolated points, could not sustain the interplay.

*Olam HaTikkun* is built specifically to enable robust Or Chozer — the Partzufim are structured so that each sefirah can both receive Or Yashar and generate a full Or Chozer without shattering.
