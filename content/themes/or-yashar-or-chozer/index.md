# אור ישר ואור חוזר — Direct and Reflected Light

**The Foundational Metaphor of the Hemshech Taarav**

This is the hub page for the Or Yashar / Or Chozer theme cluster. The distinction between direct light and reflected light — אור ישר ואור חוזר — is the organizing metaphor that runs throughout all of Hemshech Taarav. The Rashab (Rabbi Shalom DovBer Schneersohn) deploys it as a structural principle at every level: cosmological, psychological, and practical.
