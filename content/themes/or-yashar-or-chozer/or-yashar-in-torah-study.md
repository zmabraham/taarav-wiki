# אור ישר בלימוד התורה — Or Yashar in Torah Study

**The Or Yashar / Or Chozer Dynamic Applied to Torah Learning in Hemshech Taarav**

---

## Overview

Torah study (*limud haTorah*, לימוד התורה) occupies a unique position in the Rashab's treatment of Or Yashar and Or Chozer in Hemshech Taarav. Torah is unlike all other forms of *avodah* (divine service) in that it is the direct expression of the divine intellect — making Torah study the arena where the Or Yashar arrives in its most undiluted intellectual form. The Rashab's analysis of Torah study through this lens appears across multiple maamarim in the hemshech.

---

## Torah as Or Yashar Par Excellence

The Rashab develops the teaching that **Torah is Or Yashar from the highest possible source**. The Talmudic teaching "The Holy One Blessed Be He and the Torah are one" (אורייתא וקודשא בריך הוא חד הוא) is interpreted in the Taarav to mean that Torah is literally the expression of the divine intellect — Chochma Ila'ah (the supernal Wisdom). When a person studies Torah, Or Yashar from the level of Chochma flows into them.

This makes Torah study qualitatively different from prayer:
- In prayer, the soul generates Or Chozer by ascending toward God
- In Torah study, the divine wisdom descends into the human intellect — Or Yashar flowing directly into the mind of the learner

---

## The Or Chozer of Torah Study

Yet Torah study also has its Or Chozer dimension — and here the Rashab makes a crucial distinction:

**Passive reception** (merely reading or memorizing Torah): Or Yashar flows in but the Or Chozer is minimal — the student hasn't genuinely engaged their own intellect

**Active intellectual engagement** (*amkut*, depth; *chidush*, novelty): When the student wrestles with the Torah, generates their own insights, or asks probing questions, they generate Or Chozer. The student's intellectual effort *rises upward* and carries the Torah concept to its source, creating a unique elevation — the student's newly generated understanding adds something that the Or Yashar alone did not contain

The Rashab draws a remarkable conclusion: a Torah *chidush* (novel insight) — a genuine new understanding arrived at through human intellectual effort — is Or Chozer in the realm of Torah. It reaches heights that the original Or Yashar of that Torah teaching, as it existed before the student engaged with it, did not reach.

---

## Torah Study vs. Prayer: Complementarity

In the Taarav's framework, Torah and prayer serve complementary functions in the Or Yashar / Or Chozer circuit:

| | Torah Study | Prayer |
|--|--|--|
| Primary mode | Or Yashar into the intellect | Or Chozer from the soul |
| Starting point | God's wisdom descending | Human yearning ascending |
| Goal | Internalizing divine intellect | Reaching divine presence |
| Analogue | Or Yashar | Or Chozer |

Neither is complete without the other. The Rashab echoes the teaching that one who prays without Torah study has no vessel for what they receive; one who studies without prayer has no upward movement to elevate what they learn.

---

## Torah Study and Adam Kadmon

At the deepest level of the Taarav's metaphysics, the Rashab connects Torah to *Adam Kadmon* (Primordial Man) — the highest spiritual world above Atzilut. Torah study, at its most elevated, doesn't merely generate Or Chozer within Atzilut; it connects the soul to the Torah's source above Atzilut, in Adam Kadmon. This is why Torah study is called *equal to all other mitzvot combined* — its Or Yashar source is beyond all the worlds.
