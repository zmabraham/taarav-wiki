# אור ישר בתפילה — Or Yashar in Prayer

**The Application of the Or Yashar / Or Chozer Framework to Tefillah in Hemshech Taarav**

---

## Overview

Prayer (*tefillah*, תפילה) is the arena in which the dynamics of Or Yashar and Or Chozer become most vivid and personally accessible. The Rashab in Hemshech Taarav does not treat prayer as a mere ritual obligation but as a *cosmic act* — the soul's participation in the fundamental dynamic of the universe. In the maamarim throughout the hemshech, prayer is analyzed as the human enactment of the Or Yashar / Or Chozer circuit.

---

## Or Yashar Flowing Into Prayer

Before the worshipper can ascend, there must first be a descent of Or Yashar into the soul — the divine grace that enables prayer in the first place. The Rashab teaches that authentic tefillah begins not with the worshipper's effort but with the Or Yashar that *draws* the soul toward prayer:

- The very desire to pray is itself an Or Yashar — God drawing the soul toward him
- The words of the siddur carry Or Yashar encoded in them by the Men of the Great Assembly (Anshei Knesset HaGedolah)
- The structured ascent through Pesukei D'Zimra, Shacharit, and Amidah corresponds to the Or Yashar flowing through successive levels of the soul

The Rashab analyzes the structure of the Chabad daily prayer (*nusach Ari*) as a formal mapping of Or Yashar descending into the structure of the day and the soul's faculties.

---

## The Ascent as Or Chozer

The primary movement of tefillah — the *ratzo* (running/yearning) aspect — is itself the Or Chozer generated by the soul in response to the Or Yashar of divine calling:

**Pesukei D'Zimra**: Or Chozer of Asiya and Yetzira — the soul's vital and emotional response
**Shacharit / Yotzer Or**: Or Chozer of Beriya — the intellectual soul stirring
**Shema and its blessings**: Or Chozer touching Atzilut — the soul declaring and experiencing divine unity
**Amidah (Shemoneh Esrei)**: The peak of Or Chozer — direct standing before the divine presence

The Rashab emphasizes that the *kavanah* (intention) in prayer is precisely the mechanism of Or Chozer generation. Without genuine inner engagement — even in the most formally correct prayer — the Or Chozer is truncated. With full *hisponenut* (contemplative focus), the Or Chozer of prayer reaches the highest levels.

---

## The Descent After Prayer as Or Yashar

After the ascent of prayer (ratzo), the worshipper returns to the world (*shov*) — but carries back with them the Or Yashar they received *as a result of their Or Chozer*. This secondary Or Yashar is different from the initiating Or Yashar: it has been "earned" by the soul's Or Chozer response. The Rashab calls this the *pnimiyut* (internalized) light that prayer instills in the soul for the remainder of the day.

This is why the Alter Rebbe and the Rashab both insist that prayer must be followed by time (*after prayer reflection*) — to allow the Or Yashar generated by the soul's Or Chozer to become genuinely internalized.

---

## Prayer as the Central Practice of Or Chozer Generation

Among all the forms of *avodah*, the Rashab singles out prayer as the primary generator of Or Chozer because:

1. Prayer is addressed directly to the Ein Sof — its Or Chozer reaches the highest source
2. Prayer engages the entire soul — Nefesh through Yechida participate in the ascent
3. Prayer is daily — the Or Yashar / Or Chozer circuit is refreshed every morning, ensuring the soul does not become static

See: [[themes/ratzo-veshov/ratzo-veshov-in-prayer|Ratzo v'Shov in Prayer]] for a complementary analysis.
