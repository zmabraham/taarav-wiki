# Location of Each Soul in the Body

## English Explanation

One of the distinctive teachings in the Hemshech Taarav, developed from earlier
Chabad texts, is the identification of the physical location of each soul within the
body. The *nefesh habehamit* (animal soul) has its primary seat in the *heart*
(לֵב, lev) — specifically in the left chamber of the heart (חֲלַל שְׂמֹאל, chalal
smol), from which it circulates through the entire body via the blood.

The *nefesh ha'elokit* (divine soul) has its primary seat in the *brain* (מוֹחַ,
mo'ach) — specifically, the three chambers of the brain (corresponding to the three
intellectual faculties of Chochmah, Binah, and Da'at), from which it radiates
downward through the body.

This physical distinction reflects the souls' essential characters: the animal soul,
rooted in the heart, operates primarily through emotion and instinct — the drives
of love, fear, anger, desire. The divine soul, rooted in the brain, operates primarily
through intellect — understanding, wisdom, and knowledge of the divine. In spiritual
practice, the Rashab emphasizes that the divine soul's victory in the internal battle
depends on the brain maintaining governance over the heart (מֹחַ שַׁלִּיט עַל הַלֵּב,
moach shalit al halev): rational, divine awareness controlling emotional, animal-soul
impulses.

The heart is not simply negative, however. The Rashab notes that the divine soul also
has an emotional dimension, rooted in its intellectual faculties and expressed through
love of God and awe of God — but these divine emotions *follow* from intellect, unlike
the animal soul's emotions which precede and resist it.

## Hebrew Key Terms

| Term | Transliteration | Definition |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| לֵב | Lev | Heart; seat of the animal soul |
| מוֹחַ | Mo'ach | Brain; seat of the divine soul |
| מֹחַ שַׁלִּיט עַל הַלֵּב | Moach Shalit al HaLev | Brain governs the heart; the goal of avodah |
| חֲלַל שְׂמֹאל | Chalal Smol | Left chamber of the heart; animal soul's primary seat |
| שְׁלֹשָׁה מוֹחִין | Shelosha Mochin | Three brains (CHaBaD); divine soul's primary seat |
