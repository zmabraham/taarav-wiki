# נפש הבהמית — Nefesh HaBehamit: The Animal Soul

**Hub page for the Nefesh HaBehamit theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The Nefesh HaBehamit (animal soul) is the seat of human passions, drives, and ego. In Hemshech Taarav, the Rashab provides the deepest Kabbalistic grounding for the two-soul framework — revealing the animal soul's metaphysical roots and the mechanism of its transformation.
