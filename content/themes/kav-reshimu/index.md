# קו ורשימו — Kav and Reshimu

**Hub page for the Kav and Reshimu theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The Kav and Reshimu are the two post-Tzimtzum elements that together generate all of spiritual reality. The Rashab's analysis of their nature and relationship is among the most original and rigorous contributions of Hemshech Taarav.
