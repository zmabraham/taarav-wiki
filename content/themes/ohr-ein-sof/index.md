# אור אין סוף — Or Ein Sof: The Infinite Light

**Hub page for the Or Ein Sof theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The Or Ein Sof — the infinite divine light — is the ultimate source of all existence in the Hemshech Taarav. The Rashab's sustained analysis of Or Ein Sof, its modes, and its relationship to creation represents some of the most philosophically rigorous thinking in all of Chabad literature.
