# צמצום — Tzimtzum: The Contraction

**Hub page for the Tzimtzum theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The Tzimtzum — the primordial withdrawal of Or Ein Sof — is one of the central pillars of the Hemshech Taarav. The Rashab provides the most rigorous philosophical treatment of Tzimtzum in Chabad literature, resolving debates between earlier authorities and advancing the analysis of what the Tzimtzum means for creation, the soul, and spiritual practice.
