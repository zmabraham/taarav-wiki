# אבא ואמא — Abba and Ima: Father and Mother

**The Intellectual Partzufim in Hemshech Taarav**

---

## Overview

**Abba** (אבא — Father) and **Imma** (אמא — Mother) are the Partzufim corresponding to *Chochma* (Wisdom) and *Bina* (Understanding) respectively. They form the intellectual pair of Atzilut — the "parents" whose union produces the development of Ze'ir Anpin (the emotional Partzuf) and ultimately Nukva. In Hemshech Taarav, the Rashab analyzes Abba and Imma in extensive detail as the cosmic source of all divine teaching and spiritual nurturing.

---

## Abba — The Father Partzuf

**Abba** is the Partzuf of *Chochma* — divine wisdom in its most concentrated, most transcendent form. As "Father," Abba has the quality of:

- **Initiating**: Abba is the source — the "point" from which all understanding flows
- **Concealed**: Like a father's seed, Abba's wisdom is present but hidden — it requires Imma's development to become expressible
- **Bitul**: Abba retains the quality of self-nullification (*bitul*) that characterizes Chochma — Abba does not "assert itself" but transmits its quality to Imma for development

The soul's *Chaya* level corresponds to Abba — the dimension of the soul that is most directly connected to the flash of divine insight.

**Abba and Torah**: The Rashab teaches that Torah's intellectual content — the *p'shat* (simple meaning) and *derush* (homiletical) levels — derives primarily from the Abba-level. When a person grasps a new Torah understanding, they are, in a sense, receiving a flash of Abba-light.

---

## Imma — The Mother Partzuf

**Imma** is the Partzuf of *Bina* — divine understanding in its full, developed, expressible form. As "Mother," Imma has the quality of:

- **Nurturing**: Imma receives Abba's seed and develops it — like a mother who carries and nourishes the child until it is ready to be born
- **Revealed**: Unlike Abba (hidden Chochma), Imma's understanding is expressible and communicable
- **Teshuvah**: The Rashab connects Imma to *teshuvah* (repentance/return) — Bina's expansive understanding encompasses even one's past, allowing genuine return

The soul's *Neshama* level corresponds to Imma — the dimension of the soul that processes divine insight and develops it into articulated wisdom.

**Imma and learning**: When a person studies Torah and develops their understanding — moving from the initial flash of grasping an idea (Abba/Chochma) to full understanding (Imma/Bina) — they participate in the Abba/Imma dynamic within their own intellect.

---

## The Union of Abba and Imma

The *yichud Abba v'Imma* (union of Abba and Imma) is one of the most significant inter-Partzuf relationships in the Taarav. Their union produces the *mochin* (minds/mental lights) for Ze'ir Anpin — the cognitive capacity that ZA requires to function properly.

The Rashab analyzes this union as the cosmic model for genuine intellectual creativity: when the spontaneous insight (Abba) meets the developing understanding (Imma) in genuine interaction, the result is a *new* intellectual product — the mochin of ZA — that neither alone could produce.
