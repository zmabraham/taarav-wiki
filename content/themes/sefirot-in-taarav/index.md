# הספירות בתערב — Sefirot in Hemshech Taarav

**Hub page for the Sefirot theme cluster in Hemshech Taarav**

The Sefirot are the foundational structure of Kabbalistic thought, and Hemshech Taarav provides the most systematic treatment of them in Chabad literature. The Rashab analyzes the Sefirot's inner structure, their cosmic history (Tohu vs. Tikkun), their organization into Partzufim, and the specific nature of each Partzuf.
