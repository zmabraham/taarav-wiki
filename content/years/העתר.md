# שנת העת"ר (1909–10)

**חלק שני (Part 2)**

---

## Overview

The year **העת"ר** (1909–10) saw the delivery of **58 maamarim** as part of the Hemshech Taarav. The Rashab (Rabbi Shalom DovBer Schneersohn, 5th Rebbe of Chabad-Lubavitch) continued his multi-year exposition of the deepest levels of Kabbalistic-Chassidic thought, building systematically on the discourses of prior years.

## Thematic Progression

Each year of the hemshech advances specific conceptual themes. This year's maamarim continued developing the interplay between:

- **אור ישר ואור חוזר** (direct and reflected light)
- **ספירות דאצילות** (sefirot of the world of Atzilut)
- **עולמות התוהו והתיקון** (worlds of Tohu and Tikkun)
- **נשמות ישראל** (the souls of Israel and their divine roots)
