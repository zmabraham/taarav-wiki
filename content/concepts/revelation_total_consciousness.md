# The Revelation of Total Consciousness

---

## Overview

The giving of the Torah is framed as the cosmic event that forged the ontological connection between the Or Yashar (direct light) and Or Chozer (reflected light), enabling a new unified state of divine-human consciousness.

- **מתן תורה**
- **אור ישר ואור חוזר**
- **התגלות**
