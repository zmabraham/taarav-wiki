# Descending Keter Through Or Yashar

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the hemshech by establishing how the highest divine light (Keter) is channeled into physical reality through the mechanism of direct, unadulterated light (Or Yashar), linking the divine will to practical mitzvot.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אוֹר יָשָׁר**
- **כֶּתֶר**
- **דִּירָה בַּתַּחְתּוֹנִים**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The performance of mitzvot serves as the conduit for direct divine light to descend from Keter, creating a dwelling place for the Divine in the lowest realms.
