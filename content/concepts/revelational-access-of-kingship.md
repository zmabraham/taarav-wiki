# Revelational Access of Kingship

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the idea that divine kingship (Malchut) is the specific Sefirah through which the infinite becomes relationally accessible without compromising transcendence, and its heightened revelation on Rosh Hashanah is not a change in God but a greater accessibility to finite perception.
