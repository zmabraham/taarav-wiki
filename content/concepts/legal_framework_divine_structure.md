# Legal Framework as Divine Structure

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the concept that the laws and judgments are not merely social regulations but the structural blueprint for manifesting divine order within the world.

### Hebrew Terms

- **משפטים**
- **חוקים**
- **משפט**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330102]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

Parashat Mishpatim is analyzed as the establishment of a 'dwelling place for the Divine,' where human legal systems create a framework for the infinite to be contained within the finite.
