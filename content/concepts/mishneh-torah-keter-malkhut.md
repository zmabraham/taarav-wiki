# Mishneh Torah: Keter–Malkhut Communication Chain

---

## Overview

Sefer Devarim as 'Mishneh Torah' — the second Torah — reveals the Kabbalistic chain by which Keter (direct divine speech in the third person) becomes Malkhut (the emanated word that Moshe speaks in the first person and that can be received below).

### Hebrew Terms

- **משנה תורה** (Mishneh Torah)
- **כתר ומלכות** (Keter and Malkhut)
- **מדבר בגוף ראשון / שלישי** (first-person / third-person speech)

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

The shift from God's third-person speech in the first four Books to Moshe's first-person speech in Devarim is mapped as the Keter-to-Malkhut chain: the highest divine self-disclosure restructured into a form receivable by lower worlds.
