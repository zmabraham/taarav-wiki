# Transcendent Immanence

---

## Overview

This maamar introduces the concept of divine service operating within a state of transcendent immanence, where the rituals of repentance (Teshuva) are performed within the very context of God's infinite presence, making the boundary between Creator and creation permeable.

### Hebrew Terms

- **תשובה**
- **הצמדות**
- **מקום**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The discourse on Shabbat Teshuva establishes that the service of Teshuva must be performed while maintaining the awareness that one is simultaneously within God's infinite presence.
