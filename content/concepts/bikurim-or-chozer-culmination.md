# Bikurim: Or Chozer's Culminating Ascent

---

## Overview

The bikurim (first-fruits) declaration models the metaphysics of 'arrival' (bi'ah): the soul's sustained biru'rim in the lower world culminates in an upward movement that touches Keter, transforming the descent-origin into an offering of Or Chozer.

### Hebrew Terms

- **ביכורים** (first-fruits)
- **ביאה / הגעה** (arrival / reaching the destination)
- **ארמי אובד אבי** (Aramean sought to destroy my father — the descent-origin)
