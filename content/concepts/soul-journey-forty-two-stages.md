# Soul Journey Through Forty-Two Stages

---

## Overview

The soul's avodah progresses through forty-two differentiated stages of divine light, corresponding to the forty-two wilderness stations and the forty-two-letter divine Name — each stage a distinct configuration of Or Yashar and Or Chozer.

### Hebrew Terms

- **מסעות** (journeys/stages)
- **שם מב** (forty-two-letter Name)
- **גלות וגאולה** (exile and redemption)

### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330126]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

The hemshech maps the soul's spiritual journey as a structured progression of forty-two stages, showing that each stage of divine service corresponds to a specific mode of divine light — exile and redemption as phases of Or Yashar and Or Chozer.
