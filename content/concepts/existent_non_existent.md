# Existent Non-Existent

---

## Overview

The paradoxical concept of a primal state that simultaneously exists and lacks defined reality, serving as the potential for all subsequent creation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **תהו**
- **מציאות**
- **ברא**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330084]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The conceptual advance is resolving the tension between 'created' (bara) and 'was tohu' by framing 'tohu' not as non-existence but as a state of undifferentiated potentiality that precedes and enables structured existence.
