# Aromatic Sanctification

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the concept of sanctification through fragrance, presenting the 'scent of Noah's offering' as a paradigm for how the righteous transform their essence into a force that purifies and sweetens the divine judgment, turning curse into blessing.

### Hebrew Terms

- **ריח ניחוח**
- **קללה**
- **פדיון**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The narrative of God smelling the Noah sacrifice is reinterpreted to establish that the righteous emit a spiritual fragrance that serves as an atonement, transforming God's wrath into mercy.
