# Progeny as Process

---

## Overview

The understanding that the 'generations' (תולדות) of a righteous individual refer to the unfolding process of their divine service, not just their biological offspring.

### Hebrew Terms

- **תולדות**
- **תהלך**
- **איש צדיק**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330085]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The main advance is reinterpreting 'These are the generations of Noach' from a genealogical record to a metaphysical description of the righteous individual's life as a continuous process of divine interaction and revelation.
