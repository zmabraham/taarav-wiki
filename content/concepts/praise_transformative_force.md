# Praise as Transformative Force

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the concept that praise and acknowledgment are not passive compliments but an active, transformative force that subdues negativity and reveals hidden strength.

### Hebrew Terms

- **יודוך**
- **ידך בעורף**
- **הילול**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330096]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The text explains that the brothers' praise of Yehudah is what empowers him to 'place his hand on the neck of his enemies,' establishing praise as an active tool for overcoming opposition.
