# Externalization of Inner Light

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the concept that the external placement of the Chanukah lamp signifies the necessity of bringing an inner, transcendent light into the external, physical domain.

### Hebrew Terms

- **נר חנוכה**
- **חוץ**
- **שמאל**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330094]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The specific placement of the Chanukah lamp outside and to the left teaches the principle of internal divine light becoming manifest in the external, mundane world.
