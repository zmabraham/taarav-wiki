# Sacred Contraction

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the concept of Tzimtzum (contraction) as an active devotional posture, where the Sukkah represents a self-nullification before God that creates a sacred space by contracting the ego to allow the divine infinite to dwell within it.

- **השענות**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain


- **Advances**: [[concepts/grace_filled_praise|Grace-Filled Praise]]
This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The difficulty with the law of the Sukkah under the tree is resolved to teach that the act of dwelling in the Sukkah is a metaphysical act of contraction, drawing down the infinite into the finite space.
