# Divine Service Beyond Intellectual Discernment

---

## Overview

Two modes of divine service are distinguished: one rooted in bein tov l'ra (intellectual discernment of good and evil), and a higher mode that transcends the entire framework of judgment — corresponding to the two kinds of Or Yashar that descend.

### Hebrew Terms

- **ראה** (see — direct perception)
- **בין טוב לרע** (discernment of good and evil)
- **ברכה וקללה** (blessing and curse — the two modes)
