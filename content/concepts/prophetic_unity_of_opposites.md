# Prophetic Unity of Opposites

---

## Overview

Haazinu's song reveals how prophecy integrates divine judgment and mercy into a single, harmonious perspective, demonstrating that opposites can be one.

### Hebrew Terms

- **שירה**
- **צדק ומשפט**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330080]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The conceptual advance is that prophecy creates a higher reality where apparent contradictions like justice and mercy are resolved into a unified, transcendent view.
