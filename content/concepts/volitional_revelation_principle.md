# Volitional Revelation Principle

---

## Overview

Divine revelation is predicated not on compulsion but on the innate, volitional movement from creation to draw near to the Creator, establishing the true foundation for relationship.

### Hebrew Terms

- **זִרְקָא**
- **נַפְשָׁא**
- **קַרְבָּה**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330107]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

This maamar advances the core principle that divine revelation originates from within creation's own desire to connect, not from an external imposition, establishing the basis for all authentic spiritual experience.
