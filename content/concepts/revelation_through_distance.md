# Revelation Through Distance

---

## Overview

Devarim's retelling of the law reveals that true understanding and connection to God are achieved not from immediate proximity but through intellectual and spiritual distance.

### Hebrew Terms

- **משנה התורה**
- **רחוקה**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330072]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The conceptual advance is that divine revelation is more profound when mediated through a framework of separation and interpretation, as seen in Moses' second retelling of the Torah.
