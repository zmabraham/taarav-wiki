# Grace-Filled Praise

---

## Overview

This maamar develops the concept of divine praise as a response to experienced grace, where the Hallel is not a cause but an effect, a natural overflow of the soul's recognition of God's kindness, thereby making the praise itself a vessel for further divine grace.

### Hebrew Terms

- **חסד**
- **הלל**
- **הילול**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The textual question of why the nations praise God is answered to establish that true praise is an echo of experienced grace, and the act of praise actually draws further grace into the world.
