# Physical Mitzvot Carry Atzmus More Fully Than Spiritual Lights

---

## Overview

Torah opens with creation (not the first commandment) because this establishes the ontological priority of physical mitzva-performance: the physical world's Yesh carries koach ha'atzmus (the power of Atzmus) more completely than even the highest spiritual lights — physical mitzvot are superior to Torah study in accessing divine essence.

### Hebrew Terms

- **מצוות מעשיות** (physical commandments)
- **כח העצמות** (the power of Atzmus)
- **יש הנברא** (created being — the physical world as vessel of Atzmus)

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

The hemshech's most far-reaching statement: the physical world (Yesh hanivra), by virtue of its very concreteness, is a more complete vessel for Atzmus than the spiritual lights. This grounds the hemshech's final synthesis — why the world was created and why Torah begins with Bereishit.
