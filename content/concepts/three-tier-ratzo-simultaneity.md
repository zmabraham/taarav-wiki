# Three-Tier Ratzo: Simultaneous Standing Before God

---

## Overview

A three-level typology of ratzo (ascending movement) mapped onto the community grades at Nitzavim: all levels — from tribal heads to woodcutters — stand simultaneously before God on Rosh Hashana, proving that Or Chozer from the lowest level is structurally necessary to activate the divine pleasure of dira betachtonim.

### Hebrew Terms

- **ג' בחי' רצוא** (three modes of ratzo)
- **נצבים** (standing simultaneously)
- **דירה בתחתונים** (divine dwelling in the lower worlds)
