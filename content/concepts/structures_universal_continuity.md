# Structures of Universal Continuity

---

## Overview

This maamar reveals that the world's existence is maintained by three interconnected divine structures: Torah as the cognitive framework, service as the energetic bridge, and charity as the ontological foundation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **עמודי העולם**
- **תורה ועבודה וגמילות חסדים**
- **השתלשלות**
