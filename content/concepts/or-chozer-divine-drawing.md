# Or Chozer As Divine Drawing

---

## Overview

This maamar establishes the Or Chozer (reflected light) not merely as a spiritual rebound but as the precise mechanism by which human action in the lowest realms actively draws down the highest divine will (Keter) to achieve divine habitation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **Or Chozer**
- **Keter**
- **Dira BaTachtonim**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The Or Chozer dynamic is revealed as the active conduit through which lower worlds draw supreme divine light into creation.
