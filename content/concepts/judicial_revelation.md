# Judicial Revelation

---

## Overview

This maamar introduces the concept of divine judgment as an act of revelation, where the inherent concealment of God is paradoxically disclosed through the process of judgment, establishing a new ontological framework for understanding divine presence in creation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **דין**
- **הגלוי**
- **הסתתרות**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The progression from the verse in Shoftim reveals that divine judgment is not merely a process of adjudication but is fundamentally an act of revelation that transforms God's concealed nature into an expressed reality.
