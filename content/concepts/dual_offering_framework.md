# Dual Offering Framework

---

## Overview

The progression from 'תרומה' to 'תרומתי' reveals a distinction between a contribution from free will and a divinely designated portion, establishing a foundational structure for spiritual service.

### Hebrew Terms

- **תרומה**
- **תרומתי**
- **לב טהור**
