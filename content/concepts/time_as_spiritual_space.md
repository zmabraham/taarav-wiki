# Time as Spiritual Space

---

## Overview

The seven weeks between Passover and Shavuot are not merely a duration but a consecrated spiritual space that is prepared day by day to receive divine revelation at Sinai.

### Hebrew Terms

- **שֶׁבַע שַׁבָּתוֹת**
- **תְּמִימוּת**
- **עוֹמֶר**
