# The Cosmic Dialectic

---

## Overview

This maamar presents Korach's challenge as a necessary dialectical process within the divine system, representing the tension between egalitarian unity and hierarchical structure.

### Hebrew Terms

- **קרח**
- **מחלוקת**
- **סליקה**
