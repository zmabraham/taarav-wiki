# Reflection as Divine Dwelling

---

## Overview

This concept resolves the tension between direct and reflected light by showing that the ascent of Or Chozer is not merely a return but an active process through which creation becomes a vessel for Dira BaTachtonim.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אוֹר חוֹזֵר**
- **דִּירָה בַּתַּחְתּוֹנִים**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330032]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The maamar advances the hemshech by proving that reflected light is the essential mechanism through which the infinite will is concretized within a finite world.
