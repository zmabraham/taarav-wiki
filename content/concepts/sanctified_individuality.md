# Sanctified Individuality

---

## Overview

Pinchas' zealous act reveals how individual initiative, when rooted in divine passion, becomes the vehicle for ultimate revelation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **קנאה**
- **פנחס**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The hemshech advances the concept that divine revelation can be actualized through the passionate, zealous dedication of a single individual who transcends ego.
