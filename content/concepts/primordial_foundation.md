# Primordial Foundation

---

## Overview

The notion that the patriarchs, as the 'Rock' of creation, provide the essential, unshakeable foundation upon which the divine plan for the world is established, overcoming the primordial chaos.

### Hebrew Terms

- **מראש צורים**
- **האבות**
- **צור גדול**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330041]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The maamar's question about 'from the Rock of your fathers' advances the hemshech by revealing the Patriarchs as the primordial 'Rock' that grounds the cosmic order, providing the stability needed for the world's creation against the turbulent 'waters' of evil.
