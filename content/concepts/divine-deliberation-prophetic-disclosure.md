# Divine Inner Deliberation Preceding Prophetic Disclosure

---

## Overview

The grammatical peculiarity 'v'Hashem amar' (and God said, with conjunctive vav) reveals the inner divine deliberation that precedes the descent of Or Yashar into prophetic consciousness — Avraham's intimacy as 'God's friend' is what makes him the subject of this deliberation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **וה' אמר** (and God said — inner deliberation preceding speech)
- **המכסה אני מאברהם** (Shall I conceal from Avraham?)
- **אהבת אברהם** (Avraham's love/intimacy — the basis for divine disclosure)

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

The hemshech's account of prophetic disclosure as a structured divine process: Or Yashar does not simply descend but is preceded by an inner divine deliberation (v'Hashem amar) — love (ahavah) is the structural prerequisite for the channel of prophetic reception to open.
