# Dual Divine Truth

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the hemshech by positing that divine truth can be authentically apprehended through two opposing modalities—Gevurah (restriction) and Chesed (expansion)—each producing valid but divergent legal interpretations, reflecting different Sefirot refractions of a single divine reality.

### Hebrew Terms

- **חסד וגבורה**
- **דברי אלהים חיים**
- **מחלוקת לשם שמים**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The hemshech progresses by establishing that two conflicting Torah perspectives can both be 'words of the living God,' each emanating from a distinct divine attribute yet converging on absolute truth.
