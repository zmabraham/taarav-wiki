# Locational Divine Revelation

---

## Overview

The concept that divine light and revelation are contingent upon the specific structural context or 'location' within the supernal realms, determining how and where the light becomes manifest.

### Hebrew Terms

- **פני המנורה**
- **מול פני המנורה**
- **נר האמצעי**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330038]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The textual difficulty regarding the menorah's lights serves to establish that the revelation of divine light is not uniform but is predicated on the spatial relationship to a central point, suggesting that divine immanence requires a specific framework.
