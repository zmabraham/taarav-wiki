# The One Singular Desire: Dwelling in God's House

---

## Overview

The Maggid of Mezeritch's teaching on David's 'one thing I ask': dwelling in God's house is the single, all-encompassing desire from which all other spiritual fulfillment flows automatically — the Or Chozer concentrated into a single pure longing.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אחת שאלתי** (one thing I ask)
- **שבתי בבית ה'** (dwelling in God's house)
- **לחזות בנועם ה'** (beholding God's pleasantness)

### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330132]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

The hemshech demonstrates that Or Chozer reaches its most refined form not in multiplicity of spiritual aspirations but in a single, pure ratzo — the longing for divine dwelling — from which all other spiritual achievements emerge as natural consequences.
