# Divine Service as Uplifting

---

## Overview

The service of God involves the conscious and active effort of elevating the physical and material to a spiritual plane.

### Hebrew Terms

- **עבודה**
- **העלות**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330065]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The core conceptual advance is that divine service is defined as the *function* of lifting and elevating, making it an active process of spiritual ascent rather than static fulfillment.
