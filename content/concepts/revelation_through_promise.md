# Revelation Through Promise

---

## Overview

This maamar presents the idea that divine revelation is not arbitrary but is contingent upon the vessel created by prior commitment and promise.

### Hebrew Terms

- **וארא**
- **ברית**
- **השם**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330098]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The name 'Elokim' revealed at the burning bush signifies a mode of divinity that is revealed in accordance with the covenant and promises made to the Patriarchs.
