# Ascent of Lower Will

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the hemshech by elucidating how the 'lower will' of creation's physical reality can ascend, initiate its own reciprocal light, and thereby draw down the 'direct light' of Keter into the 'dwelling below.'

### Hebrew Terms

- **Or Chozer**
- **Keter**
- **Dira BaTachtonim**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330026]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The maamar's chiddush is that physical mitzvot are not just passive recipients of divine light but can actively provoke an ascent of reflected light, thereby drawing the highest direct light into the lowest realm.
