# Ascent of Or Chozer

---

## Overview

Matan Torah established a new cosmic pattern where reflected light (Or Chozer) could ascend to the highest divine levels, creating a permanent channel for infinite light to dwell within finite creation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **Or Chozer**
- **Matan Torah**
- **Dira BaTachtonim**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

Sinai enabled the Or Chozer to ascend to unprecedented heights, creating the permanent channel for divine infinity within finity.
