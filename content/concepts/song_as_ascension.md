# Song as Ascension

---

## Overview

This maamar develops the idea that the song of redemption is not a celebratory reaction but an active force that facilitates the transition from exile to redemption.

### Hebrew Terms

- **שירה**
- **אז ישיר**
- **הולך ולול**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330100]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The textual repetition of 'az yashir' establishes that the song itself is an act of ascent, carrying the singer from the physical plane to a higher spiritual reality.
