# Unified Collective Consciousness

---

## Overview

The gathering on Rosh Hashanah reveals a state where all individual distinctions are nullified, creating a single, unified vessel capable of receiving divine revelation without hierarchy or separation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **נצבים כולכם**
- **שוה**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The conceptual advance is the nullification of all individual differentiations within the collective to achieve a state of perfect equality necessary for divine coronation.
