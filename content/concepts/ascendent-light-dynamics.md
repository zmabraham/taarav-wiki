# Ascendent Light Dynamics

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the hemshech's argument by detailing how the specific actions of the human soul in the lowest realm (Asiyah) draw down divine light from the highest source (Keter), revealing the Or Chozer mechanism as the essential process for achieving divine habitation.
