# Soul's Exodus Process

---

## Overview

The monetary atonement (אשם) represents the soul's necessary journey of extraction from corporeal confines to achieve true revelation and connection with the divine.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אשם מכסף**
- **גאולה**
- **מצרים**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330105]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

This maamar advances the concept that the process of divine service requires a deliberate extraction from physicality, akin to the Exodus from Egypt, represented by the specific laws of the asham.
