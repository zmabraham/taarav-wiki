# Essential vs. Attribute Kingship

---

## Overview

The distinction between God's essential infinity and His attribute of kingship resolves the paradox of how an infinite Being can be 'crowned' by a finite creation.

### Hebrew Terms

- **עצמות**
- **הכתרה**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330078]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

This maamar introduces the conceptual advance that divine kingship is not an attribute of God's essence but a self-limiting Hevel that allows for relationship with creation.
