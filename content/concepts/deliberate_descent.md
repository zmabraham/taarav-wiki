# The Concept of a Deliberate Descent

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the concept that the seeming descent into servitude under Pharaoh was itself a conscious act of divine will to facilitate a greater ascent.

### Hebrew Terms

- **בא**
- **מכת**
- **עבדות**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330099]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The plagues are understood not just as punishments but as a process of 'descending in order to ascend,' where the descent into Egyptian bondage is a prerequisite for a higher level of redemption.
