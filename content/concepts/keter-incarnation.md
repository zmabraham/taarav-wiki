# Keter's Incarnation

---

## Overview

This concept explains how the performance of a mitzvah serves as the vessel that draws the highest light from Keter down into the lowest reality, making Dira BaTachtonim a tangible reality.

### Hebrew Terms

- **כֶּתֶר**
- **דִּירָה בַּתַּחְתּוֹנִים**
- **הֶמְשֵׁךְ**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330028]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The maamar's chiddush is that a mitzvah is the precise mechanism through which Keter's light is actively drawn down, transforming abstract divine potential into a divine dwelling in the physical world.
