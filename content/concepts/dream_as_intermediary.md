# Dream as Intermediary

---

## Overview

The concept that the dream state serves as a crucial metaphysical intermediary between the conscious, waking self and the divine realm.

### Hebrew Terms

- **ויצא**
- **חלום**
- **מקום**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330090]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The conceptual advance is establishing the dream as a structured gateway, a 'place' (מקום), that allows for a unique form of divine communication that bridges the gap between the material and spiritual worlds.
