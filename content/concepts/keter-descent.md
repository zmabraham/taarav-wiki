# Keter's Descent

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the hemshech by establishing how the highest Keter-level light becomes consciously drawn into the lowest reality through Torah performance.

### Hebrew Terms

- **כֶּתֶר**
- **אוֹר יָשָׁר**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The parasha's mitzvah demonstrates the mechanism by which Keter's direct light is made to dwell in the physical world.
