# Soul's Seven Gates as Structured Divine Interfaces

---

## Overview

The seven sense-portals of the embodied soul are structured interfaces between the human person and supernal reality — not merely physical apertures but loci where divine light must be governed by judgment (mishpat) to function as channels rather than breaches.

### Hebrew Terms

- **שערים** (gates / portals)
- **שופטים ושוטרים** (judges and officers)
- **ז' שערים שבנפש** (seven soul-gates)
