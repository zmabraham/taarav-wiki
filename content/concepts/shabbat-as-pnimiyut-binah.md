# Shabbat as the Pnimiyut of Binah

---

## Overview

Shabbat is identified as the 'seventh sefirah from below' — the pnimiyut (innermost dimension) of Binah — the time when all seven levels of existence ascend beyond their ordinary modes of operation into their source.

### Hebrew Terms

- **פנימיות הבינה** (pnimiyut of Binah)
- **שמור / זכור** (guard / remember)
- **עלייה** (ascent of all levels)

## Position in the Conceptual Chain


- **Advances**: [[concepts/heel-level-connects-keter|The Heel Level Connects to Keter]]
Shabbat's dual command — 'guard' (passive, receptive Or Chozer) and 'remember' (active, initiatory Or Yashar) — is resolved by identifying Shabbat with Binah's pnimiyut: the point where all distinctions between direct and reflected light dissolve into their supernal root.
