# The Measurement of Sacred Potential

---

## Overview

The maamar develops the concept of 'Naso' (elevating/summing) as the act of precisely measuring and elevating the latent divine potential within the world, particularly through the tribe of Levi.

### Hebrew Terms

- **נשא**
- **העלאה**
- **פוטנציאל**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330120]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

Divine service involves the precise measurement and elevation of the world's spiritual potential, transforming raw reality into a vessel for divine manifestation.
