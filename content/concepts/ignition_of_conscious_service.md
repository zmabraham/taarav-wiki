# Ignition of Conscious Service

---

## Overview

This maamar introduces the concept of 'Beha'alotcha' (when you kindle) as the initial spark of conscious intention that ignites the entire process of divine service, transforming passive potential into active worship.

### Hebrew Terms

- **בהעלותך**
- **הדלקת נר**
- **כוונה**

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The kindling of the Menorah represents the primordial act of conscious intent that must precede all divine service, serving as the ignition that propels the soul's journey from potential to actualization.
