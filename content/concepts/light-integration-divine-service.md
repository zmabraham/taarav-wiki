# Light Integration in Divine Service

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the discussion by revealing how physical mitzvot actively draw Or Yashar from Keter into our world, creating a dynamic reciprocity where divine light is both poured down and reflected back up.

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The performance of Torah law is the mechanism that channels the highest divine light into the lowest reality, completing the cosmic circuit of creation.
