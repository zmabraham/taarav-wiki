# Flood and Teiva: Or Yashar Overwhelming, Or Chozer Preserved

---

## Overview

The flood (mabul) typifies the total inundation of Or Yashar overwhelming the keilim (vessels) of creation; Noah's ark (Teiva) is the protected interior within which Or Chozer is preserved — corresponding to the dual Shabbat/Rosh Chodesh structure of weekly Or Chozer and monthly Or Yashar renewal.

### Hebrew Terms

- **מבול** (flood — overwhelming Or Yashar)
- **תיבה** (ark — preserved Or Chozer interior)
- **ראש חודש** (Rosh Chodesh — monthly Or Yashar renewal)

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

The Noach narrative provides the typological frame for the Or Yashar/Or Chozer dynamic: when Or Yashar flows without the vessels' capacity to receive it, it 'floods' and destroys — the ark's function is precisely to preserve the Or Chozer process within the overwhelming flow.
