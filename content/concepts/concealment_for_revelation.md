# Concealment for Revelation

---

## Overview

The necessity of initial concealment as the necessary condition for a more potent and complete revelation of the divine.

### Hebrew Terms

- **הסתרה**
- **גילוי**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330059]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The maamar introduces the idea that concealment is not merely the absence of revelation but an active, preparatory stage that enables a more elevated disclosure of the divine essence.
