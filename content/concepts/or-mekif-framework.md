# Or Mekif Framework

---

## Overview

This maamar establishes the Or Mekif (surrounding light) as the exclusive modality for divine habitation within the temporary Sukkah, contrasting it with the Or Pnimi (inner light) of permanent structures, thereby positioning temporary, encompassing divine service as the ultimate channel for connecting to the soul's Chaya-level.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אור מקיף**
- **אור חוזר**
- **כתר**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The Sukkah's temporary structure uniquely facilitates an encounter with Or Mekif, allowing the soul to access its deepest Chaya-level potential through a service that encompasses rather than directly internalizes the divine presence.
