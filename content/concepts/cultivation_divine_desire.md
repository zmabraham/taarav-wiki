# Cultivation of Divine Desire

---

## Overview

The perpetual, kindled fire on the altar represents the soul's own inherent, ever-active desire to serve and connect with the divine, which must be consciously nurtured.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אֵשׁ תָּמִיד**
- **עֲבוֹדָה**
- **חֲשַׁקְתִּי**

## Position in the Conceptual Chain


- **Advances**: [[concepts/deficiency_as_foundation|Deficiency as Foundation]]
This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The maamar advances the concept that true divine service is not a one-time act but the continuous cultivation of an inner, self-generating fire of passion and connection.
