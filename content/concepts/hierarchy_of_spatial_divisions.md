# Hierarchy of Spatial Divisions

---

## Overview

The boundaries between spiritual and physical realms are established through a hierarchical system of divisions, each with a specific divine purpose.

### Hebrew Terms

- **גבולות**
- **הבדלה**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330067]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

This maamar posits that the separation between different levels of reality is not random but is structured in a specific hierarchy, with each level's purpose defined by its position within this cosmic order.
