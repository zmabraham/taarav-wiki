# Counting the Soul Fragments

---

## Overview

The maamar introduces the paradox of counting the Jewish people, revealing it as an act of gathering and integrating the 'dust' (fallen fragments) and 'quarters' (core essence) of the collective soul.

### Hebrew Terms

- **עפר יעקב**
- **רובע ישראל**
- **פירוק והרכבה**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330125]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The census is a profound metaphysical act that reassembles the scattered fragments of the divine soul, integrating both its fallen material elements and its essential divine core into a whole.
