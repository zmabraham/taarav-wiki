# Divine Light Infusion

---

## Overview

This maamar advances the argument by detailing how the downward flow of Or Yashar becomes activated through the upward movement of Or Chozer, thereby drawing the highest divine consciousness into the lowest realms of practical action.

### Hebrew Terms

- **אוֹר יָשָׁר**
- **אוֹר חוֹזֵר**


### Maamarim That Advance This Concept

- [[maamarim/maamar-3601330034]]

## Position in the Conceptual Chain

This concept represents a key advance in the hemshech's development.

The performance of physical mitzvot transforms divine light from a passive, direct state into an active, reciprocal force that consciously enters the material world.
